Wayanad

Edakkal Caves

Edakkal caves are well known for its pictorial depictions (cave art), which are thought to be of 6000 B C. To achieve the hollows, one needs to trek through the Ambukutty Mala. It will take around 45 minutes to climb the hill

Kuruva Island

The island is sprawling across an area of 950 acres with a dense population along with some rich and rare species of flora and fauna. It protects the evergreen forest ranges which provide a different ambience
